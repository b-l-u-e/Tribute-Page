Tim Bergling

    Swedish electronic musician, DJ, and songwriter,Born in 8 September 1989 Stockholm, Sweden, known professionally as Avicii. His debut studio album, True (2013). In 2015, Bergling released his second studio album, Stories, His posthumous third album titled Tim was released in 2019.
